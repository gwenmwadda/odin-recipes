Recipe For Ice

Delicious Ice The Old Fasioned Way

Ingredients

  • 3 Sturdy Men
  • 1 talking snowman (Optional)
  • Sled
  • Ice Pick

Steps:

  1. Tell sturdy men the ice queen has gone crazy and can no longer provide refreshing ice.
  2. Pack sled.
  3. Head up the enchanted mountain.
  4. Bury the sturdy man eaten by the stray yeti.
  5. Bring back ice.
  6. Drink your now refreshing drink while telling faimly of thier lost father on the expedition.
